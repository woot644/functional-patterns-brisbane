## Why Correct Posture Matters

- **Reduced pain** — proper alignment removes strain from muscles and joints
- **Improved breathing** — optimal rib cage position allows full diaphragmatic function
- **Increased energy** — your body works more efficiently when aligned
- **Improved digestion** — posture directly affects organ function
- **Improved mood** — body position influences mental state and confidence

## Our Approach

### Root Cause Analysis
We don't just tell you to "stand up straight." We identify the underlying movement dysfunctions that pull your body out of alignment.

### Breathing Patterns
Dysfunctional breathing is both a cause and consequence of poor posture. We address breathing mechanics as a core part of postural correction.

### The Jenga Tower Analogy
Your body is like a Jenga tower — you can't fix the top without addressing the base. We look at the entire structure, not just the area that looks wrong.

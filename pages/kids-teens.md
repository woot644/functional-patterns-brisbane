## Biomechanics & Posture Training for Children & Teenagers

---

## 3 Reasons to Start Young

### 1. Catch Issues Early
Postural and movement dysfunctions are far easier to correct before they become deeply ingrained. Early intervention prevents years of pain and compensation.

### 2. Address Conditions Like Cerebral Palsy
Children with conditions such as cerebral palsy can benefit significantly from biomechanics-based training that works with their developing nervous system.

### 3. Improve Athleticism
Young athletes who train biomechanics develop better coordination, speed, and injury resilience — giving them an edge in any sport.

---

## Why Start Young?

- **Cleaner slate** — fewer years of accumulated dysfunction to work through
- **Neuroplasticity** — young nervous systems adapt faster to new movement patterns
- **Lower body mass** — less load on the system makes correction easier
- **Less inhibition** — children are more willing to move in new ways without overthinking

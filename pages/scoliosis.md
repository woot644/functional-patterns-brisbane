## Treatment Approach

Our scoliosis treatment protocol addresses the condition through multiple pathways:

- **Myofascial release** — releasing fascial restrictions that hold the spine in dysfunctional patterns
- **Nervous system calming** — down-regulating the nervous system to allow structural change
- **Gait & posture rebuilding** — retraining how you walk and hold yourself to create lasting spinal correction
- **Inflammation reduction** — addressing the chronic inflammation that accompanies scoliosis
- **Dietary protocols** — nutritional strategies to support tissue healing and reduce inflammation
- **Lifestyle interventions** — modifications to daily habits that reinforce correction

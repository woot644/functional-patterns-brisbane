## How Gait Analysis Works

Our gait analysis identifies the specific dysfunctions in your walking and running mechanics:

- **Excessive dropping** — pelvic drop and hip collapse during gait
- **Stiffness** — areas of restricted movement that force compensation
- **Spinal curvature** — how your spine behaves during dynamic movement
- **Rotation failures** — inability to properly rotate through the trunk and pelvis

# Trainer Credentials

**URL:** https://www.functionalpatternsbrisbane.com/trainer-credentials

---

## Minimum Requirements

All practitioners hold a minimum of **Certificate III & IV in Fitness** as their baseline qualification.

---

## Additional Qualifications

Team members bring a range of additional qualifications including:

- Myotherapy
- Remedial massage
- Physiotherapy/osteopathy backgrounds
- Diplomas in related health fields
- University studies in health science and related disciplines

---

## HBS Pathway (Human Biomechanics Specialist)

The HBS certification is the Functional Patterns-specific credential pathway that practitioners progress through:

### HBS1
- Gait analysis and correction
- Rib cage-pelvis relationship
- Breathing mechanics and correction

### HBS2
- Rotational movement patterns
- Multi-planar training and correction

### HBS3-4
- Advanced biomechanics assessment and correction
- Mentoring and development of other practitioners

## Research Articles by Louis Ellery

### 1. Ankle & Foot Mechanics
Research summary covering the role of ankle and foot mechanics in whole-body movement and how dysfunction at the foot level drives compensations up the kinetic chain.

### 2. Arm-Swing in Running
Analysis of how arm-swing mechanics influence running efficiency, injury risk, and overall gait quality.

### 3. Hip Internal Rotation
Research on the importance of hip internal rotation for gait, athletic performance, and pain prevention.

### 4. Gait Retraining for Knee Pain
Summary of evidence supporting gait retraining as an effective intervention for chronic knee pain.

### 5. Movement vs Standard Exercise
Comparison of movement-based training approaches versus standard exercise protocols for pain and dysfunction.

### 6. Diaphragm & Spine Stability
Research on how diaphragm function directly influences spinal stability and chronic pain.

### 7. Gait, Hip & Pelvis
Analysis of the relationship between gait mechanics, hip function, and pelvic alignment.

### 8. Fascia & Contraction
Research on fascial contraction mechanisms and their implications for movement, pain, and training.

### 9. Motor Control vs Strengthening
Summary comparing motor control approaches with traditional strengthening for pain and dysfunction resolution.

## Chronic back pain is rarely just a "back problem." It's a movement problem.

---

## Assessment

Our assessment uses **slow-motion gait and posture analysis from multiple angles** to identify the movement dysfunctions driving your chronic pain. We look at how your entire body moves — not just where it hurts.

---

## Training Elements

- **Chambers/correctives** — targeted exercises to address specific dysfunctions
- **Dynamic progressions** — building toward functional, real-world movement
- **Myofascial release** — releasing tissue restrictions that hold pain patterns in place
- **Rib-pelvis alignment** — restoring the relationship between your ribcage and pelvis
- **Hip stability** — building the foundation for pain-free movement
- **Breathing mechanics** — correcting dysfunctional breathing patterns that drive pain

---

## Protocol

1. **90-minute initial assessment** — comprehensive gait and posture analysis with corrective training
2. **1-2 weekly 60-minute sessions** — ongoing training to rebuild movement patterns
3. **Homework** — exercises and corrections to practice between sessions for faster results

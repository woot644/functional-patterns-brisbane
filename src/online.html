The Methodology

What Is FP Online Training?

Our online sessions use the Functional Patterns methodology. A results-driven approach to improving posture, gait, and movement efficiency — without cookie-cutter exercises or temporary fixes.

Our certified Human Biomechanics Specialists identify and correct root causes of pain and imbalance through tailored drills and progressions via live Zoom sessions.

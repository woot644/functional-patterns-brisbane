In-Person Practitioners

Meet the Team

Every practitioner at FP Brisbane is trained in the Functional Patterns methodology — combining gait analysis, biomechanics, and corrective strength training.

LE

Louis Ellery

Owner & HBS

Owner of FP Brisbane with a background in physiotherapy. After suffering multiple injuries during cricket in England — including Achilles tendinopathy, rotator cuff tears, and lumbar stress reactions — Louis discovered Functional Patterns in 2015. He has been pain-free for years and built FP Brisbane into the city's leading biomechanics practice with 130+ five-star reviews and 15 practitioners.

Currently not taking new clients

SA

Skye Ashton

HBS1 Certified

After 9 years teaching Comprehensive Pilates, Skye recognised most approaches only manage pain rather than resolve it. Her own back and hip issues led her to Functional Patterns in 2023. Now HBS1 certified, she no longer requires regular physical therapy and helps clients correct the movement patterns driving their pain.

SM

Sam Marsh

HBS1 Certified

Sam discovered Functional Patterns 6 years ago while practising movement systems that were damaging his body. With a study background in evolutionary biology and human health, he brings a holistic perspective to biomechanics correction. At 30, he moves better than ever and is passionate about solving complex, multifactorial problems.

KZ

Keriann Zipperer

HBS1 Certified

Holds a Bachelor of Health Science (Clinical Nutrition) and HBS1 certification. Keriann came from a conventional gym background before discovering FP revealed her training and movement patterns were entirely nonfunctional. Five years later, she is more symmetrical, no longer hypermobile, and brings a unique nutritional and hormonal health perspective to biomechanics training. Currently on maternity leave.

ES

Emmanuel Searty

HBS1 Certified

5+ years dedicated practice with a background in tennis, Muay Thai, and soccer combined with organic farming experience. After chronic stiffness and recurring injuries that stretching only worsened, Emmanuel found Functional Patterns transformed everything. He specialises in rebuilding fundamental movement patterns and helping clients achieve lasting structural change.

EK

Emma Kopcikas

She brings both clinical expertise and lived experience to every session.

HBS Certification Pathway

The Human Biomechanics Specialist (HBS) credential is the Functional Patterns-specific pathway that practitioners progress through:

1

HBS1

Gait analysis & correction, rib cage-pelvis relationship, breathing mechanics

2

HBS2

Rotational movement patterns, multi-planar training & correction

3

HBS3

Advanced biomechanics assessment & correction

4

HBS4

Mentoring & development of other practitioners

Strength Training Built for Women

This isn't your typical group fitness class. Bells & Bands is a progressive, biomechanically-informed strength program designed to help women build core stability and glute activation — without the intimidation of a traditional gym environment.

Using dumbbells and resistance bands, each session focuses on controlled, purposeful movement that strengthens the muscles that matter most for posture, pelvic stability, and long-term pain prevention.

Is This For You?

Who This Program Is Designed For

✦

Hypermobility & Joint Pain

Women dealing with hypermobility or chronic joint pain who need structured, safe strengthening.

✦

Postpartum Returners

Mums returning to exercise after pregnancy who want to rebuild core and glute function properly.

✦

Beginners Welcome

Women new to strength training who want a supportive, low-pressure environment to get started.

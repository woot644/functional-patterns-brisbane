What You Get

6 Components of the Assessment

Full Posture Analysis

Comprehensive assessment of your postural alignment from multiple angles to identify asymmetries and imbalances.

Gait Breakdown

Detailed analysis of how you walk and move, filmed from 4 angles in slow motion to reveal hidden dysfunctions.

Movement Screening

Identifying dysfunctional movement patterns, muscular atrophy, weakness and compensations throughout your body.

Root Cause Diagnosis

Tracing your symptoms back to their true origin rather than just treating the site of pain.

Personalised Correction Plan

A targeted plan to address your specific issues with a detailed roadmap for lasting correction.

Education That Makes Sense

Clear explanation of what's happening in your body and why, so you understand the path to recovery.

What You Get

6 Components of the Assessment

Full Posture Analysis

Identify compensations, rotations, and load faults in your muscle chains.

Gait Breakdown

See exactly how your walking pattern influences your pain. We break it down in a way you can understand.

Movement Screening

We assess your rib cage, pelvis, foot mechanics, breathing, sling systems & more during walking and running.

Root Cause Diagnosis

We identify why the issue exists rather than treating the symptoms. Less labels, more answers.

Personalised Correction Plan

We discuss your starting drills, priorities, and next steps. You will try these in the initial session.

Education That Makes Sense

Understand your body in a way that actually empowers change.

Your First Session

90-Minute Session Breakdown

Every minute is purposeful. Here's exactly what happens during your initial assessment.

1

Gait Analysis

Filmed from 4 angles in slow motion to capture every detail of how you walk and move.

2

Posture Analysis

Assessed from 4 angles to identify postural imbalances and structural asymmetries.

3

Real-Time Gait & Posture Breakdown

See exactly what's happening when you move. We walk you through your biomechanics so you understand the full picture.

4

Assessment Results Discussion

Clear findings from your evaluation presented in plain language. No jargon, no confusion.

5

Thorough Plan Development

A detailed roadmap for your correction based on your unique findings, tailored to your goals.

6

45+ Minutes Corrective Training

Hands-on corrective work in your very first session. You leave with exercises and a clear direction.

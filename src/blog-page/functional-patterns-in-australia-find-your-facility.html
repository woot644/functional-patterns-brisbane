Functional Patterns in Australia — Find Your Facility

Written by Louis Ellery • 2026-04-29

Functional Patterns has quietly built a significant presence across Australia, with certified facilities now operating in every major city and several regional areas.

Founded in 2006 by Naudi Aguilar, Functional Patterns is a biomechanics-based training methodology that works backwards from the way humans actually move — standing, walking, running, and throwing. Rather than isolating muscles, practitioners analyse gait, posture, and fascial tension to address the root cause of chronic pain and dysfunction.

Every certified facility currently operating in Australia

New South Wales

Functional Patterns Sydney — Alexandria, Sydney NSW

One of Australia's most established FP facilities, FP Sydney offers both one-on-one and group training. Specialising in biomechanics assessment, posture correction, and pain-free movement, the facility works with clients across all fitness levels and physical backgrounds.

functionalpatternssydney.com

Victoria

Functional Patterns Melbourne — Brunswick, Melbourne VIC

Based in Brunswick, FP Melbourne is known for its thorough gait and posture assessments and its team of Human Biomechanics Specialists. The facility takes on complex cases — scoliosis, sciatica, post-surgical rehabilitation — in a welcoming, non-judgmental environment.

functionalpatternsmelbourne.com

Functional Patterns Surf Coast — Surf Coast Region, VIC

Serving the Surf Coast and Geelong region, this facility brings the full FP methodology to regional Victoria, specialising in postural alignment, functional movement retraining, and nutrition education.

functionalpatternssurfcoast.com

Queensland

Functional Patterns Brisbane — East Brisbane, QLD

Queensland's flagship FP facility, FP Brisbane has an exceptional track record with clients who've exhausted other options — chronic pain, degenerative conditions, post-surgical cases. Their slow-motion gait footage analysis is a hallmark of the assessment process.

functionalpatternsbrisbane.com

Functional Patterns Sunshine Coast — Sunshine Coast, QLD

FP Sunshine Coast works with a wide range of clients, from those recovering from long-term sciatica to runners wanting to eliminate pain from their training.

functionalpatternssunshinecoast.com

Western Australia

Functional Patterns Perth — Perth, WA

WA's leading biomechanics facility, with a particular emphasis on scoliosis correction and structural postural work. Fully certified and verified with Functional Patterns global.

functionalpatternsperth.com

All facilities are certified and in good standing with Functional Patterns. You can verify any practitioner's certification via the official locator at functionalpatterns.com/pages/find-a-facility

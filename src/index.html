Our Approach

We Treat Your Body as the Whole Unit That It Is

Unlike many pain management clinics, we analyse your gait and posture to find the root cause — not just treat the symptom site.

  • ✦ Find asymmetries and dysfunctional movement patterns
  • ✦ Identify muscular atrophy, weakness and compensations
  • ✦ Correct through functional strength training
  • ✦ Improve posture, reduce inflammation, prevent future injury
